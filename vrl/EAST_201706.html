Commercial Catch by Zone

Plot variable:


Spatial breakdown of key fishery statistics in the current fishing season. Selectable as total commercial catch, fishing effort or standardised CPUE. Colour shading is weighted relative to each indicator. Top right hand box displays detailed information.

Commercial Catch History


Total annual catch (kg) in the Rock Lobster Eastern Zone fishery from 1978/79 to present, displayed by financial year. Horizontal line indicates the Total Allowable Catch (TAC) between 2009/10 and present. TAC was introduced to the fishery in 2001/02, but for the first eight years was based on fishing season (Nov-Sept) and not financial year.

Historical TAC Data

Annual Catch / Effort Data

Region:


Total annual catch (tonnes) and effort (# pot lifts) selectable by region (Queenscliff, San Remo and Lakes Entrance) and for all of fishery. Data displayed by fishing season (Nov-Sept).

Length Frequency Distribution

Year: Survey: Sex:


Length frequency of rock lobsters sampled in the observer and fixed-site surveys. Displays four sequential years, with most recent year selected under the "Year" dropdown. Selectable by sex (male/female) and survey type (observer/fixed-site). Dashed line is legal minimum length.

Average Weight


Average weight in kg of rock lobster caught each fishing season (Nov-Sept), selectable by region (Queenscliff, San Remo and Lakes Entrance) and for all of fishery. Dashed line is series average.

Fixed Site Survey

Subgroup:


CPUE for rock lobster caught at fisheries-independent fixed-site surveys, selectable by sex (male/female) and size (juvenile/adult). Data displayed by fishing season (Nov-Sept). These surveys use pots with escape gaps open. Dashed line is series average

Observer Survey

Subgroup:


CPUE for rock lobster recorded by on-board observers, selectable by sex (male/female) and size (juvenile/adult). Data displayed by fishing season (Nov-Sept). These surveys use regular commercial pots with escape gaps open. Dashed line is series average.

Monthly Data Trends

Plot variable:


Monthly update of key fishery statistics for the current and previous quota season (Jun-July). Selectable as monthly catch, cumulative catch, or monthly CPUE. Data presented is for the whole fishery. TAC for current year is indicated by a dashed grey line.

Standardised CPUE


Annual time series of standardised catch-per-unit effort (CPUE) (kg/potlift), plotted by region.

Relative Abundance of Recruits


Relative abundance of recruits (<60mm), as estimated by the rock lobster model, from 1978/79 to present.

Modelled Egg Production


Model estimated Rock lobster egg production, as percentage of the estimated 1951 level. Dashed line is the limit reference point from the Harvest strategy.

Estimated Biomass & Exploitation


Model estimated available biomass and proportional exploitation in the Rock lobster fishery from 1978/79 to present.
